Kotake Kuzuryu

Kotake Kuzuryu (九頭龍 小竹 Kuzuryu Kotake) is a former member of the Beach who specialized in diamond games.

Appearance:

Kotake is a young girl of average build, with dark hair and eyes. She typically wears athleisure or comfortable and modest clothing.

Personality:

Kotake is shown to be a well-mannered young girl who remains level-headed even during incredibly pressuring situations. Calculating and rational, she possesses a natural talent for reading other people and possesses an extensive knowledge of many topics, which she often uses to her advantage during games. Confident and proud of her abilities, Kotake can sometimes be seen as arrogant by other people, whom she looks down upon for not being as efficient as her. However, due to her somewhat isolated upbringing due to her parents' respective careers, she is desperately starved for affection and came to believe that the only way she can make others care for her was to stand out in every aspect of her life, hence her overachieving nature. She enjoys when others praise her abilities, especially her violin-playing, which she has devoted almost all of her time towards perfecting. She also is shown to enjoy romance novels designed for self-inserts. Despite her intelligence, she is still a child, and her immaturity shows when it comes to dealing with people, such as not communicating her needs effectively and getting upset when others don't understand what she wants. She is quite talkative when prompted to speak about her interests, contrary to her father's more solemn disposition, and she has a suggestive imagination and a somewhat immature sense of humor. In the end, she is a kind girl at heart who hopes to help the people she cares about succeed in the ways she is familiar with.

History (Backstory)

Kotake was born out of wedlock to Mr. Kuzuryu and his girlfriend at the time, while the two were still in law school. Due to their insufficient means to take care of a child at the time, Kotake was left in the care of her grandparents, who spoiled her, and paid for her violin instruction. At some point in her life, Kotake found out about the circumstances of her birth, leading her to feel the need to compensate for her own existence by means of academic and extracurricular success. This led to the development of her arrogant personality, pushing away friends and acting coldly towards those she deems unworthy of her time and effort.

Relationships:

  • Mr. Kuzuryu (father) - Kotake and her father have a distant relationship, due to the nature of his work as an international lawyer preventing him from spending much time with her. He cares enough about her to leave her in the care of her grandparents, and has remembered important dates in her life and sent gifts. He has also shown some reluctance in testing her in a game, however discards it in favor of observing her mental prowess and fortitude for his own studies regarding human nature.
