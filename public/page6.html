Overview

  Zacharie comes from the name "Zachariah", a name of Hebrew origin meaning "The Lord recalled". Finley is a surname of Irish, Gaelic, and Scottish origin meaning "Courageous one/Fair Hero". His ethnicity is Norwegian, but he may be mixed because of his father.


  His online handle, "mesentericaRobot" is a play on both his major in biology (mycology) and online status. Mesenterica is the species of an edible fungi commonly referred to as "Witches' Butter" (scientific name: Tremella Mesenterica) which is gelatin-like but tough when wet, and also has a saturated yellow-orange color. It could also refer to "Mesenteric", a word that means relating to the mesentery, a membrane that connects the bowel to the back abdominal wall. Robot on the other hand generally refers to a machine that automatically carries out actions/commands programmable by a computer. This is actually a reference to the slang term "robot" on 4Chan (an anonymous forum site created in 2003) about a board called "ROBOT9000" (r9k) which is infamous for its controversial topics and socially awkward users/posters commonly referred to as "robots".
