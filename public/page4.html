Appearance:

He has an exaggeratedly branch-like crooked nose, and the placement of his eyes are obviously asymmetrical (his left eye being obviously higher-placed up his face than his right one which falls around his cheek-bone area). His posture is uneven, and he looks like a hunch-back underneath his long dark coat. His skin colors look like block shading and rough coloring. In contrast to everything else about his warped-up appearance, his eyes are actually quite normal, and they have a light to each pupil, showcasing a form of innocence and determination.


Personality:

Kafka is a humble, but insecure man- not taking pride in his appearance, especially with a twin sister so adventurous and charming. He is a perfect balance for her however, as he is very imaginative, and sensitive. He is both comforted and disturbed by the fact that he resembles his grandfather in a few ways both in terms of appearance and personality. Unlike Grout however, he definitely doesn’t want to be looked down upon by the higher-ups (most prominently Turn Gregor, who has emotionally belittled him, and groomed him). He is not as confident as the comical Burlesque nor as adored as the gorgeous Picturesque, but he finds a way to contribute to this revolt by being influential in his literary works under the alias “Huckery”.


History (Backstory)

There is an alien species referred to as ‘Discordians’, split up into multiple tribes. These tribes are very hostile and weary of each other, typically reacting with violence and lashing anger towards another when done so. As time went on however, this hostility seemed to gain some formality, and some tribes began to grow tolerant of each other, and became allies to gain better odds against their personal enemies. His tribe is very down-to-earth, simple, and compared to other Discordian tribes, have a long history of avoiding feuds, and being underdogs to the society being built. Another thing that adds to their more naturally humble nature is the unity they have compared to other tribes, as well as their long years of being under the Turn tribe as colonies.

Trivia:

  • Despite being named “Kafka”, he is actually inspired by the life and the artworks of Vincent Van Gogh.
  • - The (prominent) Esque tribe members in the story are all named after words that end with the “-esque” suffix, and their personalities are based on the definitions respectively.

  • - Discordians format their name as “[tribe name] [given name]”.

  • - Each of the Esque tribe members’ appearances are based off a random object that one may find in their homes- for the example of Kafka, he is based off an old photo/painting/book covers, which explains his disproportionate appearance which may also be another metaphor for “being used” just like an object.

  • - He does not struggle at writing any literary form except haikus, having even raged over it once before crying for two hours about it.

  • - The titles of most of his works (which do not go labelled under his name) contain the word/name "Huckery" to the point that other Discordian tribes have recognized it as a pseudonym.
