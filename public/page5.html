Appearance:

Zhi-Han dons an indigo-blue yī-fu training uniform, which is loose and comfortable enough to move in, and is secured via black cuffs around the knees, elbows, and a black belt around the waist. He has long white hair that is fixed into twin braids, unnaturally rising and bending over his head to resemble the antennas of an insect, more specifically, inspired by a mantis. He has a middle-gray skin tone, which is common compared to the wide variety that the Eppur tribe has, ranging from pure white to pure black.


Personality:

He is focused and dedicated, demonstrating a strong work ethic and a commitment to getting the job done right. However, this serious demeanor shifts when he’s with companions and those he trusts. He generally can’t help that he’s a silly man, albeit a proud one at that considering his tribe’s high status compared to other fellow Discordian tribes. He is a cheery and agreeable man, but he does recognize to make guests remember that they are but guests.


Abilities:

  • - Ability to control his interactions with objects (Newton's 2nd and 3rd Laws of Motion). Manipulating force/acceleration and friction.

History (Backstory)

There is an alien species referred to as ‘Discordians’, split up into multiple tribes. These tribes are very hostile and weary of each other, typically reacting with violence and lashing anger towards another when done so. As time went on however, this hostility seemed to gain some formality, and some tribes began to grow tolerant of each other, and became allies to gain better odds against their personal enemies. His tribe is respected and looked upon highly for their physics-related abilities, as well as their respectable culture, however, he finds it difficult to get out of bed in the morning, typically slipping on the ground because he forgets to let himself feel friction.

Trivia:

  • - The name “Zhì” (智) by itself means “ambition, wisdom”, and is a fire-elemental name; “Hán” (韓) means “brave, ferocious”, and is a water-elemental name. The name has phonetic similarity to “志涵”, a name that means “ambitious” and “thoughtful”.

  • - Discordians format their name as “[tribe name] [given name]”.

  • - The braids of his hair are bent like antennas, and they even occasionally twitch when his touch senses are more responsive.

  • - His fighting style and appearance is inspired by a real life Chinese fighting style referred to as “Praying Mantis”, which heavily emphasizes close-range fighting, and quick movements and techniques. This is what earns him the nicknames (insults) “Buckle-wrists” from Turn Gregor, and “Lineola” (The scientific name for an African Praying Mantis) from Kitten Gatzo.

  • - The braids of his hair are bent like antennas, and they even occasionally twitch when his touch senses are more responsive. This is what earns him the nickname (insult) “Twitchy” from Ambrosia Ophidia.
